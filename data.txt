AI & I by Every — "The AI Sandwich: Where Humans Excel in an AI World"
AI & I（Every 出品）——《AI 三明治：人类在 AI 世界的优势所在》

The central metaphor of this episode is the "AI sandwich": humans are the bread, AI is the filling.
本期核心比喻是"AI 三明治"：人类是面包，AI 是馅料。

Humans are indispensable at two moments — framing the problem at the start, and polishing the output at the end; the middle execution layer is largely automated.
人类在两个时刻不可或缺——开始时定义问题框架，结尾时打磨输出；中间的执行层已基本自动化。

Kieran Klassen, GM of Quora and creator of Compound Engineering, describes a four-step workflow — plan, work, review, compound — where the "compound" step feeds learnings back into the repo so agents don't repeat mistakes.
Quora GM 兼 Compound Engineering 创始人 Kieran Klassen 描述了一个四步工作流——计划、执行、审查、复利——其中"复利"步骤将学习成果反馈到代码仓库，让 agent 不再重复犯错。

The "work" phase is essentially solved; where humans still add irreplaceable value is judgment, taste, and frame-setting.
"执行"阶段已基本解决；人类仍能提供不可替代价值的地方是判断力、品味和框架设定。

Klassen draws a parallel to musical composition: the beginning (ideation) and the end (performance) are deeply human; the middle (practice) is mechanical and can be delegated.
Klassen 将其类比为音乐创作：开始（构思）和结尾（演奏）是深度人类化的；中间（练习）是机械性的，可以委托出去。

Dan Shipper argues we are far from AGI by his bar: an agent running 24/7 that is economically profitable without human steering — we're not even close.
Dan Shipper 认为，按他的标准，我们距 AGI 还很遥远：一个不需要人类引导、全天候运行且经济可行的 agent——我们甚至还差得很远。

The actionable takeaway: lean into what brings you joy and use LLMs to amplify that, rather than replacing the moments that give your work meaning.
可操作的要点：专注于让你快乐的事情，用 LLM 放大它，而不是替换那些赋予工作意义的时刻。
