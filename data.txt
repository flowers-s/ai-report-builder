### Training Data — "From SEO to Agent-Led Growth: Profound's James Cadwallader"

We've reached a point where if your marketing team is not using agents to do marketing, you are failing — it's gone from a nice-to-have to a must-have.
我们已经到了这样一个节点：如果你的营销团队没有使用智能体来做营销，你就是在失败——这已经从锦上添花变成了必备条件。

The biggest platform shift in marketing history is happening as the world moves from blue-link search to probabilistic AI responses.
随着世界从蓝链接搜索转向概率性 AI 响应，营销史上最大的平台转变正在发生。

It's not just that the front door of the internet has changed — the person going through that door has changed: consumers have been replaced by agents making discovery decisions on their behalf.
不仅仅是互联网的前门变了——走进那扇门的人也变了：消费者已被替代他们做发现决策的智能体所取代。

Unlike SEO (content designed to be picked up by an algorithm but consumed by a human), in the new world content is designed entirely to be both discovered and consumed by an agent.
不同于 SEO（内容设计为被算法抓取但由人类消费），在新世界中，内容完全是为被智能体发现和消费而设计的。

Different AI platforms behave like different species: Gemini leans heavily on YouTube, ChatGPT pulls from Reddit (B2C) or LinkedIn (B2B), while Claude is increasingly using the live web more.
不同的 AI 平台表现得像不同的物种：Gemini 大量依赖 YouTube，ChatGPT 从 Reddit（B2C）或 LinkedIn（B2B）抓取内容，而 Claude 正越来越多地使用实时网络。

On dead internet theory: within three years it may be possible that humans no longer click into websites, breaking the advertising-funded economics of the open web — and social media may become the last human-curated data layer for AI.
关于"互联网死亡"理论：三年内，人类可能不再点击进入网站，打破互联网以广告为基础的经济模式——社交媒体可能成为 AI 最后的人工策划数据层。

AI-powered advertising in conversational interfaces will be the most effective form of advertising the world has ever seen, given the rich intent data and hyper-personalization capabilities.
在对话界面中由 AI 驱动的广告，凭借丰富的意图数据和超个性化能力，将成为世界上有史以来最有效的广告形式。

The ad unit of the future is likely a system prompt: brands will brief AI on their audience, tone, and knowledge base, and let it generate personalized messaging in context.
未来的广告单元可能就是一个系统提示：品牌将向 AI 提供目标受众、语调和知识库，让其在对话上下文中生成个性化信息。
