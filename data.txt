Zara Zhang (@zarazhangrui)

A powerful reframe on what "building" actually means: when you sit down to type, that's the LAST step. 80% of writing already happened in your head.
对"构建"本质的有力重新诠释：当你坐下来开始打字时，那是最后一步。80% 的写作已经在你脑海中完成了。

Source: https://x.com/zarazhangrui/status/2048101948826751429

The parallel holds for product, design, and engineering — building product ≠ writing PRDs, designing ≠ making mockups, engineering ≠ writing code.
这个类比同样适用于产品、设计和工程——做产品不等于写 PRD，做设计不等于画原型，写代码不等于搞工程。
