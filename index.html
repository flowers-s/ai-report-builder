MIT 教授 Heather Kulik 解释了为什么材料科学缺少属于自己的"AlphaFold 时刻"——该领域比蛋白质折叠复杂得多，设计空间远更庞大，真实数据也更难获取。 她介绍了自己的实验室如何用机器学习加速发现用于碳捕获、电池和药物递送的新材料——不是替代物理规律，而是大幅缩小搜索空间。 核心洞察：最大的瓶颈不是模型质量，而是数据。大多数失败的材料实验从未发表，导致训练集中存在巨大的选择性偏差。
LATENT SPACE：THERE IS NO ALPHAFOLD FOR MATERIALS — AI FOR MATERIALS DISCOVERY WITH HEATHER KULIK MIT PROFESSOR HEATHER KULIK EXPLAINS WHY MATERIALS SCIENCE LACKS ITS "ALPHAFOLD MOMENT" — THE FIELD IS FAR MORE COMPLEX THAN PROTEIN FOLDING, WITH VASTLY LARGER DESIGN SPACES AND HARDER-TO-OBTAIN GROUND TRUTH DATA. SHE DESCRIBES HOW HER LAB USES ML TO ACCELERATE THE DISCOVERY OF NEW MATERIALS FOR CARBON CAPTURE, BATTERIES, AND DRUG DELIVERY — NOT REPLACING PHYSICS, BUT NARROWING THE SEARCH SPACE DRAMATICALLY. KEY INSIGHT: THE BIGGEST BOTTLENECK ISN'T MODEL QUALITY, IT'S DATA. MOST MATERIALS EXPERIMENTS ARE NEVER PUBLISHED IF THEY FAIL, CREATING MASSIVE SELECTION BIAS IN TRAINING SETS.
