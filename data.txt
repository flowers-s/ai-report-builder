Swyx
Swyx argued that false equivalence — treating multiple things as equally important — is a silent killer of companies and careers.
Swyx 指出，虚假等价——把多件事情都视为同等重要——是公司和职业生涯的隐形杀手。
False equivalence killed my first devtools startup. False equivalence makes you underpay your top performers and spend too much time on lost causes.
虚假等价葬送了我的第一家开发者工具初创公司，让你给顶尖人才开低薪，却在无望项目上浪费大量时间。
