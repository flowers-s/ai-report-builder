Every gave every team member their own OpenClaw — a personal AI agent — and the results fundamentally changed how the company operates.
Every 为每位团队成员配备了各自的 OpenClaw（个人 AI Agent），这一举措从根本上改变了公司的运营方式。

The core insight: a personal agent that evolves through your interactions becomes a reflection of you — your skills, personality, and judgment — creating a parallel "org chart" of specialized agents.
核心洞察：一个通过与你互动而不断进化的个人 Agent，会成为你的映射——你的技能、个性和判断力——从而形成一张平行的"专业化 Agent 组织架构图"。

A standout moment: Brandon had his agent call him during a 28-minute walk to verbally triage his inbox — without ever setting it up as a capability.
一个亮点时刻：Brandon 让 Agent 在他步行 28 分钟上班途中打电话给他，逐一语音处理收件箱——而这个能力完全是自发探索出来的。
