### Anthropic Engineering

**Quantifying infrastructure noise in agentic coding evals**
Agentic coding benchmarks like SWE-bench and Terminal-Bench are commonly used to compare frontier model capabilities—but infrastructure configuration alone can swing scores by 6 percentage points.
智能编程基准测试（如 SWE-bench 和 Terminal-Bench）常用于比较前沿模型能力——但仅基础设施配置差异就能造成 6 个百分点的分数浮动。

In internal experiments on Terminal-Bench 2.0, Anthropic found that tighter container resource limits caused more pod crashes, inflating failure rates by up to 5.8%.
Anthropic 在 Terminal-Bench 2.0 的内部实验中发现，更严格的容器资源限制导致更多 Pod 崩溃，故障率最高可达 5.8%。

The key insight: a single pinned resource spec sets the guaranteed allocation equal to the hard kill threshold, leaving zero headroom for transient spikes.
关键发现：单一固定资源配置会让保证分配量等于强制终止阈值，不留任何应对瞬时峰值的余量。

They recommend specifying both a guaranteed floor and a separate hard ceiling per task—calibrated so scores at floor and ceiling fall within noise of each other.
他们建议为每个任务分别设定保证下限和独立的硬性上限——并校准确保两者分数差异在噪声范围内。

Takeaway for benchmark consumers: leaderboard differences below 3 percentage points deserve skepticism until eval configuration is fully documented.
对基准测试使用者的建议：在评测配置完整记录之前，3 个百分点以内的排行榜差异应持怀疑态度。

🔗 https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/infrastructure-noise

### Latent Space — "Mistral: Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & Mistral 4 — w/ Pavan Kumar Reddy & Guillaume Lample"

Mistral released Voxtral TTS, their first text-to-speech audio model—a 3B-parameter model built on the Ministral backbone with a novel auto-regressive flow-matching architecture.
Mistral 发布了 Voxtral TTS，这是他们的首个文字转语音音频模型——一个基于 Ministral 主干、采用新颖自回归流匹配架构的 30 亿参数模型。

The model uses an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic), enabling high quality at a fraction of the cost of competitors.
该模型使用内部自研的神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 潜在令牌（语义 + 声学），以远低于竞争对手的成本实现高质量输出。

Unlike depth-transformer approaches that require K autoregressive steps per frame, Voxtral's flow-matching head cuts inference down to 4–16 steps—crucial for real-time voice agents.
与每帧需要 K 个自回归步骤的深度变换器方法不同，Voxtral 的流匹配头将推理步骤减少到 4-16 步——这对实时语音智能体至关重要。

Guillaume Lample explained Mistral's broader Forge platform: helping enterprises fine-tune models on their proprietary data using the same battle-tested training infrastructure Mistral uses internally.
Guillaume Lample 阐述了 Mistral 更广泛的 Forge 平台：帮助企业使用 Mistral 内部使用的久经考验的训练基础设施，基于其专有数据微调模型。

*"When customers use this off-the-shelf closed model, what's very sad is that they are not leveraging the data they have been collecting for years or sometimes decades."*
*"当客户使用现成的闭源模型时，非常遗憾的是，他们并没有利用多年甚至几十年积累的数据。"*

Leanstral was also discussed: Mistral's formal math proving project using the Lean proof assistant, positioned as a testbed for long-horizon reasoning research and software verification.
还讨论了 Leanstral：Mistral 使用 Lean 证明助手的形式化数学证明项目，定位为长程推理研究和软件验证的测试平台。

The team previewed Mistral 4, described as a major step in pre-training that merges capabilities from separate specialized models (coding, reasoning, vision, audio) into one unified mixture-of-experts architecture.
团队预告了 Mistral 4，被描述为预训练的重大飞跃，将独立专业模型（编程、推理、视觉、音频）的能力整合到一个统一的专家混合架构中。
